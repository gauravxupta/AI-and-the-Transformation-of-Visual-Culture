Text-to-Image: How It Works

The diffusion process — from noise to masterpiece

The Diffusion Process

  1. 01Encode Text — CLIP converts your prompt into numerical vectors
  2. 02Start with Noise — Begin with pure random Gaussian noise
  3. 03Iterative Denoising — U-Net removes noise in guided steps
  4. 04Decode Image — VAE converts latent space to pixels
  5. 05Final Output — A coherent image matching your description

Deepfakes
The Dark Side of AI

AI-generated fake videos and images that are nearly indistinguishable from reality, posing serious threats to truth and trust.

Real-World Impact

  • $25M stolen — deepfake CFO video call defrauded a British firm
  • Election interference — fake Biden robocall in NH primary
  • Financial scams — deepfake Elon Musk investment fraud

Misinformation & the Erosion of Trust

When any image can be fabricated, how do we know what's real?

The Problem

  • Liar's Dividend — real evidence dismissed as "probably AI"
  • Epistemic crisis — undermining shared reality
  • Journalism at risk — photographic proof devalued
  • Legal evidence — courts questioning visual proof

The Solutions

  • C2PA / Content Credentials — digital provenance tracking
  • AI watermarking — invisible markers in AI content
  • Detection tools — AI that detects AI-generated content
  • Media literacy — educating the public

The Ethics of AI Art

A spectrum of perspectives on AI's role in creative expression

Critics Argue

  • Theft — trained on artists' work without consent
  • Job displacement — threatening livelihoods
  • Homogenization — aesthetic monoculture
  • Devalues skill — years of practice obsolete
  • Bias amplification — reflects training data biases

Advocates Argue

  • Tool, not threat — like cameras and Photoshop
  • Democratizes creativity — everyone can create
  • New art forms — entirely new genres emerging
  • Rapid ideation — accelerates creative workflows
  • Curation matters — prompting is itself a skill

"Art is not just about the output — it's about the intention, the struggle, and the human experience behind it."
